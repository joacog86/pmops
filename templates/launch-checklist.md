# Launch Checklist — [Feature Name]

**Launch date:** [Date]  
**PM:** [Name]  
**Status:** 🔴 Not started / 🟡 In progress / 🟢 Done

---

## 4 Weeks Before Launch
- [ ] PRD signed off by engineering and design
- [ ] Success metrics defined and agreed with data team
- [ ] Legal / compliance review complete (if needed)
- [ ] Beta / preview customers identified

## 2 Weeks Before Launch
- [ ] Feature complete in staging
- [ ] QA complete — no P0/P1 bugs open
- [ ] Help article drafted (CS review)
- [ ] Release notes drafted
- [ ] Sales / CS enablement doc sent
- [ ] Internal demo scheduled (if warranted)
- [ ] Analytics events confirmed firing in staging
- [ ] Feature flag / rollout strategy defined

## 1 Week Before Launch
- [ ] Stakeholders notified of launch date
- [ ] Rollback plan documented
- [ ] Support team briefed
- [ ] Final sign-off from PM and Eng lead

## Launch Day
- [ ] Deploy to production
- [ ] Verify feature is live and working for real users
- [ ] Confirm analytics events are firing in prod
- [ ] Send internal announcement (Slack)
- [ ] Publish release notes / changelog
- [ ] Monitor error rates and key metrics for first 2 hours

## 1 Week After Launch
- [ ] Review adoption and engagement metrics
- [ ] Collect early user feedback
- [ ] Address any P1 bugs or quick wins
- [ ] Share results with stakeholders

## 1 Month After Launch
- [ ] Full metrics review vs. success criteria
- [ ] Document learnings
- [ ] Decide: iterate, expand, or sunset
